A Very Short and Basic Analysis of Some Chris Cornell Lyrics

Unlike the "basic" template, the header text is INSIDE of a .content class so it doesn't get too wide

By Nicole Choi

I’m a fan of Chris Cornell and admire his songwriting, and I wanted to try text analysis, so this project is an initial attempt at that.

I scrapped a lot of my initial questions because my brain feels a bit fried, and I’m trying to turn in this project as close to the deadline as possible (even though it’s already past due). Maybe I’ll revisit them at a later time. What I was able to answer is how the diversity of words in Chris Cornell’s changed over time and how they differed across his different projects, from Soundgarden to Temple of the Dog to his solo career to Audioslave.
